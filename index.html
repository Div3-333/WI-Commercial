The Architecture

Built on a hardened Rust core, Wrought Iron handles massive datasets without ever leaving your machine. It combines elite data cleaning, PII masking, and advanced geospatial analysis into a single, air-gapped binary.

Rust-Powered Performance: Sub-second processing on millions of rows.

Zero-Network Footprint: True air-gapped compatibility for secure facilities.
